What We Offer ?

With the highest happiness index and the least bureaucratic corruption in the region and also the political and economical stability of the country, Oman is a very good choice to expand your business into and get a mid-term or long-term residency visa.


With a thorough and versatile range of services including initial free consultation, travel dates offer, hotel booking and car renting, arranging business meetings with your potential customers and visiting your product points of sales in the market, as well as legal consultation, company and brand registration, acquiring visa and residency card of the shareholders and their families, consultation on the Oman real estates market to help you rent or buy a house or office, we have paved the way for you and would accompany you all the way through the process
